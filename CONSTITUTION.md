# Doula Constitution

## Product Promise

Doula helps families find trusted non-medical birth, postpartum, newborn, night,
and culturally fluent care through a calm marketplace and concierge matching
experience.

## Durable Rules

1. The homepage must prioritize trust, clarity, and matching intent over broad
   brand storytelling.
2. User-facing copy comes from approved content data. Missing copy is completed
   as real copy before implementation; placeholders are not allowed.
3. The product must avoid medical claims. Use "non-medical support" when scope
   needs to be clarified.
4. Images must be generated or curated as standalone assets. Do not rely on
   reference screenshot crops for production page imagery.
5. English is the active language, but the content model must allow later
   Japanese and Chinese variants without rewriting component layout.
6. Visual design follows the selected reference: pale beige base, pale
   clay-orange CTA, muted sage/olive trust accents, serif display type, and
   structured marketplace cards.

## Active Boundary

M0 is a demo homepage only. It may fake search, provider cards, FAQ, and CTA
links as static UI, but it must not pretend to complete real booking, payment,
credential checks, or medical care.
